## 3. Window coupon (direct voxel) — analytic expectations

- `shape_zyx = (12, 16, 20)` (nx=20, ny=16, nz=12 — all distinct, so any transpose is
  detectable), `voxel_size_xyz_m = (0.0005, 0.0004, 0.0003)` (anisotropic),
  `local_to_world` = translation `(0.010, 0.020, 0.030)` m.
- Palette `0 background / 1 transparent / 2 white / 3 black`; transparent matrix fills
  the coupon.
- White inclusion box `z[2:5] y[3:7] x[4:10]` ⇒ **72** cells; black marker box
  `z[8:10] y[11:14] x[15:18]` ⇒ **18** cells; transparent ⇒ **3750**; background **0**.
- Features are non-symmetric across X/Y/Z; the black marker's minimum corner is
  `material_id[8, 11, 15]`. A test confirms swapping any two axes relocates it.
- Bounds (world) `min (0.01, 0.02, 0.03)` m → `max (0.02, 0.0264, 0.0336)` m.
- Payload `sha256 = fc9c3b36…2e0b` (recorded in the committed summary; satisfies the
  Step 1 follow-up to fix the coupon digest with the Step 4 fixture).

## 4. Stepped wedge (mesh) — analytic expectations

- Watertight single solid: a 4-step staircase profile (run 4 mm, rise 2 mm) extruded
  6 mm along +Y, emitted as **36** triangles. Construction is a wall ring + two
  apex-fan caps (the region is star-shaped from the bottom-right profile vertex), giving
  a single, consistently oriented, 2-manifold solid that passes `inspect_topology`.
- Voxelized at 1 mm isotropic voxels (padding 1): `shape_zyx = (10, 8, 18)`, world
  bounds `max (0.016, 0.006, 0.008)` m.
- Per-step occupancy along X (4 cells/step): step k = `4·6·(2k)` = `{1:48, 2:96, 3:144,
  4:192}` ⇒ **480** occupied cells total. All step tops/sides fall between cell-centre
  planes, so occupancy is exact (no boundary ties).
